3DOT

3DOT Token was created to be the utility token of 3DOT NFT , a liquidity protocol originally based on the polygon network. 3DOT holders can perform some operations within the protocol, such as staking (ensuring liquidity and receiving rewards in return), giving tokens for loans (and also being rewarded for doing so) and voting on 3DOT DAO improvement decisions, since 3DOT is also a governance token.

Why should you buy the 3DOT Token?

3DOT Protocol

3DOT is a governance and utility token used within the 3DOT Ecosystem to empower a decentralized community building at the forefront of web3.

→ 3DOT was launched as the base layer to foster developments within the 3DOT Ecosystem.

→ $3DOT is the ecosystem’s governance token, allowing 3DOT holders to participate in DAO.

→ 3DOT provides access to certain parts of the ecosystem that are otherwise unavailable, such as exclusive games, merch, events, and services.

→ 3DOT is a tool for third-party developers to participate in the ecosystem by incorporating $3DOT into services, games, and other projects.
